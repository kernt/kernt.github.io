AdminerEvo works out of the box with MySQL, MariaDB, PostgreSQL, SQLite, MS SQL, Oracle, Elasticsearch and MongoDB. In addition, there are plugins for [SimpleDB](https://github.com/adminerevo/adminerevo/blob/main/plugins/drivers/simpledb.php), [Firebird](https://github.com/adminerevo/adminerevo/blob/main/plugins/drivers/firebird.php) and [ClickHouse](https://github.com/adminerevo/adminerevo/blob/main/plugins/drivers/clickhouse.php).

https://docs.adminerevo.org/
